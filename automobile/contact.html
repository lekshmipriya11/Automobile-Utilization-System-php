Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services and processes

How soon can I get an appointment?

We typically offer same-day or next-day appointments for most services. Emergency repairs are prioritized and can often be accommodated the same day.

What forms of payment do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, cash, and offer financing options for larger repairs. We also work with most extended warranty companies.

Do you offer loaner vehicles?

Yes, we have a fleet of loaner vehicles available for customers whose repairs will take more than 24 hours. Availability is on a first-come, first-served basis.

Are your technicians certified?

All our technicians are ASE (Automotive Service Excellence) certified and receive ongoing training to stay current with the latest automotive technologies.

What is your warranty policy?

We offer a 12-month/12,000-mile warranty on all parts and labor for most repairs. Specific warranty details are provided with your service estimate.
