About Red Clover

We are more than just an automobile service company - we are your trusted partner on the road

Our Humble Beginnings

Founded in 2005 by automotive enthusiast Michael Roberts, Red Clover started as a small garage with a big vision. Michael believed that car owners deserved honest, transparent, and high-quality service without the hassle and inflated prices common in the industry.

What began as a one-man operation has grown into a full-service automotive center with a team of certified technicians, but our core values remain unchanged. We still treat every vehicle as if it were our own and every customer as part of our family.

Where We Are Today

Today, Red Clover stands as a premier automotive service center, equipped with state-of-the-art technology and staffed by highly trained technicians. We've expanded our services to meet the evolving needs of modern vehicles while maintaining our commitment to personalized service.

Our team continues to pursue ongoing education and training to stay at the forefront of automotive technology, ensuring we can handle everything from classic cars to the latest electric vehicles.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional automotive services that prioritize customer satisfaction, safety, and value. We aim to build lasting relationships based on trust, transparency, and technical excellence.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted name in automotive care, setting industry standards for quality service, customer experience, and technological innovation while maintaining our community-focused values.

Our Values

The principles that guide everything we do at Red Clover

Integrity

We believe in honest recommendations and transparent pricing. If we wouldn't recommend it to our family, we won't recommend it to you.

Excellence

We're committed to the highest standards of workmanship and continuously invest in training and technology to deliver superior results.

Community

We're proud to be part of the local community and believe in giving back through sponsorships, charity events, and supporting local initiatives.

Safety

Your safety is our top priority. We never compromise on quality when it comes to ensuring your vehicle is safe for you and your family.
